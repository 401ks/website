About Our Institution

A Legacy of Nursing Excellence Since 1979

The School of Nursing, Abia State University Teaching Hospital (ABSUTH), Aba, is one of Nigeria's premier nursing institutions. Established with a vision to address the critical need for qualified nursing professionals, we have consistently maintained high standards in nursing education.

Our comprehensive curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with extensive clinical practice, ensuring our graduates are well-prepared for the challenges of modern healthcare delivery. We are proud to be fully accredited by the Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria (NMCN).

Admissions

Begin Your Nursing Journey

Admission into the School of Nursing, ABSUTH is competitive and based on merit. We seek candidates who demonstrate academic excellence, compassion, and a commitment to healthcare service.

1

Check Requirements

5 O'Level credits including English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics (not more than 2 sittings)

2

Purchase Form

Application forms available online or at the school bursary. Fee: ₦15,000 (non-refundable)

3

Entrance Examination

Sit for the entrance exam covering English, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics. Successful candidates proceed to interview.
